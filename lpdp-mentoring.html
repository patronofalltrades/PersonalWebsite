About LPDP

LPDP (Lembaga Pengelola Dana Pendidikan) is Indonesia's premier government scholarship fund, supporting thousands of Indonesian students pursuing graduate education domestically and abroad. Many LPDP recipients aspire to entrepreneurship ‚Äî but the transition from academic scholarship to building a business is a path with few guides.

The Mentoring Community

This initiative brings together experienced Indonesian entrepreneurs and LPDP alumni to mentor current scholars and young aspiring founders. The goal is simple: shorten the learning curve and give the next generation of Indonesian entrepreneurs a real advantage.

  • One-on-one mentoring: Pairing aspiring entrepreneurs with founders who've built companies from zero in Indonesia's unique market conditions
  • Community sessions: Group discussions on fundraising, product-market fit, team building, and navigating Indonesian business culture
  • Knowledge sharing: Honest, first-person accounts of what it's actually like to build a startup in Indonesia ‚Äî the wins and the hard lessons
  • Network access: Connecting mentees with investors, co-founders, and ecosystem partners across Southeast Asia

Why I Do This

Indonesia has extraordinary entrepreneurial talent, but access to mentors with real operational experience is scarce ‚Äî especially outside Jakarta. I benefited from people who were generous with their time when I was starting out, and this is my way of paying that forward. Every founder I mentor is a reminder that the most valuable thing you can give someone isn't capital ‚Äî it's clarity.
